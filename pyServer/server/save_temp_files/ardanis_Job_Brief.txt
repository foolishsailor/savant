Full Stack Developer Job Description
Ardanis Technologies Limited are looking for a skilled Full Stack Developer (.NET and Angular) to
join our team with an immediate start. This role is ideal for someone who is highly motivated to
work with our team who produce high-quality solutions for our customers. You will be a key
player in our team helping with design, development and guiding other team members. The ideal
candidate should be passionate about software development craftsmanship and be interested in
emerging technology and approaches. A hands-on approach with an eye on best practice and
processes are required for the role.

Full Stack Developer Requirements
 -5+ Years of .NET backend development experience
 -2+ Years Angular frontend development experience
 -Strong practical knowledge of JavaScript, Typescript, HTML & CSS skills
 -Strong practical knowledge of C#(or similar)
 -Experience working with IIS or other hosting models
 -Experience unit testing (Jest, Mocha, NUnit, Moq etc)
 -Experience building user interfaces with Angular
 -Experience building RESTful APIs
 -Experience working with ASP.NET Core MVC
 -CSS pre-processing such as SASS, SCSS or LESS
 -Experience working with Git and related contribution workflows (feature branches, pull requests etc.)
 -Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 -Excellent knowledge of Relational Databases, SQL and ORM technologies (Entity Framework, Dapper
etc.)
 -Experience with test-driven development and behavioural driven development.
 -Experience using SpecFlow(optional)
 -Experience with Agile Development and SCRUM
 -Experience with DevOps practices such as CI/CD, Continuous Testing and Infrastructure as Code
 -English Speaking
